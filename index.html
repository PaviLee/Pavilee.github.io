Coding Bootcamp Overview

Our coding bootcamp is a 2 month long course in which we will teach our students the fundementals of Python and how to develop basic artificial intelligence systems. The course will take place from Wednesday January 6th to Wednesday February 24th. We will be having online classes 3 days a week, on Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays. No previous experience is necessary, but a proficient level of english is required because everything will be taught in english. To be eligible, you must be enrolled in a colombian High School and be in 10th grade or above (american system).

Hackathon Overview

After our bootcamp, you are invited to put your new skills to the test in our Hackathon! At the end of the bootcamp, we will be organzing a Hackathon in conjuction with Google!
